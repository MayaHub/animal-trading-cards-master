The Desert Tortoise

The tortoise has been the official California state Reptile since 1972.

  • Scientific Name: Gopherus agassizi
  • Average length: 12 inches
  • Average Lifetime: 65 years
  • Habitat: Mojave and Sonoran Deserts

The desert tortoise moves at a solid pace of about 20 feet per minute. This patient vegetarian has existed on Earth almost unchanged for millions of years. It now enjoys protected status as an endangered species. The desert tortoise played a key role in the passage of the CA Desert Protection Act in 1994.

Sources: Wikipedia & CA State Library
